# RADLOK High-Current Battery Connector PCB Identification

*Source: epicEFI Discord, 2026-02-26 | Channel: 1432272833014272050*
*Contributors: @ZeeKay, @offtheband*

## Summary

Identification of a high-current battery connector (RADLOK family) used on ECU/power distribution boards, including the specific PCB-side part number and a datasheet reference.

## Details

### Connector Family

- Connector identified as **RADLOK** (IDS 67 RADLOK series).
- Similar/related families: **SURLOK** (keyed, non-rotating variant).
- RADLOK connectors typically use screw terminals for the PCB-side mount.

### PCB-Side Search Terms

- Search for: **"RADLOK FEED-THRU"** to find the board-mount version.
- If the connector is red and 10mm, the likely part number is: **RL9100-101-F1RE**

### Datasheet

- IDS 67 RADLOK datasheet (via Mouser): https://www.mouser.com/datasheet/2/18/1/IDS_67_RADLOK-372465.pdf

### Notes

- SURLOK connectors have a key and cannot rotate; RADLOK connectors do not have this key.
- These connectors are commonly used for high-current battery/power connections on automotive ECU boards and power distribution units.
