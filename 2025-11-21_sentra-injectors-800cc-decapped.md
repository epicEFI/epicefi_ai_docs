# Nissan Sentra Injectors as High-Flow Alternatives (800cc, Decapped)

*Source: rusEFI Discord, 2025-11-21 | Channel: general/support*
*Contributors: ggurov*

## Summary

2003–2006 Nissan Sentra injectors are a recommended high-flow injector option for rusEFI builds requiring approximately 800cc/min flow capacity. They require decapping (removing the external plastic cap/filter basket) for use in aftermarket applications. These were available on eBay at low cost at the time of the discussion.

## Details

- **Application:** 2003–2006 Nissan Sentra (stock engine injectors)
- **Flow rate:** ~800cc/min
- **Modification required:** Decapping — removal of the plastic cap/filter basket on the injector body to fit non-OEM injector seats or adapters
- **Sourcing:** Available used on eBay (ggurov shared a direct link: https://ebay.us/m/9EAFVa — note this link may expire)

**When to consider these injectors:**

- When the stock injectors are undersized for a modified or boosted application and a cost-effective high-flow option is needed.
- The 800cc flow capacity suits moderate-power forced-induction builds where larger injectors are required but exotic/expensive options are not necessary.

**Notes on decapping:**

- Decapping removes the OEM top filter/plastic retainer. This is a physical modification and is irreversible.
- After decapping, ensure the injector o-rings are in good condition and size the injector o-rings/adapters to match the intake manifold injector bores.
